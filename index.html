About Me

I am a member of Tsinghua's class of 2024 in Automation, with a minor in Statistics. I enjoy problems where perception, control, and learning meet: how embodied agents build usable representations, how world/action models support planning, and how humanoid systems can turn multimodal inputs into robust whole-body behavior.

My project experience spans discrete vision tokenizers, tactile representation for robot control, active learning for robot coaching, embodied benchmarks, deep-learning course projects, and quadruped control.
